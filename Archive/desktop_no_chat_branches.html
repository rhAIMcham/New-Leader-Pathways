Hey new leader! Welcome to your first day on the job. I hope you are settling in well, and have everything you need to succeed. As you get used to your new responsibilities, it might be helpful to consider what your goals are as a new leader.

Reflection is crucial to successfully transition into a new leadership role. There is a lot changing in your day to day, and thinking about what kind of leader you want to be is a really important step in this transition process. Some essentials steps are:

  • Establish our identity within the confines of the role
  • Acquire new knowledge and hone new skills
  • Reframe existing work relationships and foster new alliances
  • Gain credibility and respect
  • Become the leader we aspire to be, and the leader our team requires.

To help you through this process, answer these 3 questions and send them back to me, and I will make sure any extra resources or supports you need to achieve those goals are available to you.


  1. What excites you about your new role and what concerns you?
  2. As you commence in the role, what are your priorities?
  3. What challenges do you think you might face?

Ok, talk soon.

Susan
